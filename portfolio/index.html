Reseach Projects

Check out my case study of Algorithmic Biases in EDM models. Machine learning is being used in various ways in the educational domain to improve teaching and learning outcomes. One of the most important applications of machine learning in education is in the field of Educational Data Mining (EDM). EDM is a multidisciplinary research field that focuses on the development of algorithms and tools for analyzing large datasets of educational data, with the goal of improving learning. g. One of the most used EDM case is to build models to predict student success or retention. However, growth in EDM means growth in algorithmic bias, and it is imperative that we work to understand the bias that data mining models/algorithms may result in.
